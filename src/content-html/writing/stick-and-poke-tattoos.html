Stick and poke tattoos

Prior to the pandemic I was giving tattoos to my friends. Most of them using the classic stick and poke method. I love the imperfect, immature nature. I spend most of my days working to make things perfect for temporary digital mediums, so it's nice to work on something imperfect that is permanent.

Below are some tattoo designs I've created. I try to draw things that would make me laugh in 7th grade. It's crude, rude, and none of it is something you should get tattooed on your body. Ever.
