Privacy Policy for Ok Bloomer

Last Updated: June 2026

Ok Bloomer ("Ok Bloomer," "we," "our," or "us") helps users identify, collect, and document flowers through photography, field observations, and location-based exploration.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

Information We Collect

Photos You Submit

When you use Ok Bloomer to identify flowers, photos you choose to submit may be processed by our identification providers to determine flower species and related information.

Photos are uploaded only when you actively choose to identify a flower.

Account Information

If you create an account, we may collect:

  • Name or display name
  • Email address
  • Account identifiers
  • Subscription status

Journal and Collection Data

To provide field journal features, we may store:

  • Identified flower species
  • Observation history
  • Notes and journal entries
  • Collection progress
  • Achievement and badge progress
  • Regional collection information
  • Observation dates and timestamps

Location Information

With your permission, Ok Bloomer may collect location information to:

  • Associate observations with geographic regions
  • Display nearby parks and flower-viewing locations
  • Improve identification accuracy
  • Support regional collections and discovery features

Location access is optional and can be disabled at any time through your device settings.

Device Information

We may automatically collect limited technical information, including:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Anonymous diagnostic and crash information

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Identify flowers
  • Maintain your field journal and collection history
  • Provide app functionality
  • Support achievements and collection tracking
  • Improve identification accuracy
  • Process subscriptions and purchases
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve app performance and reliability

Third-Party Services

Ok Bloomer uses third-party service providers that may process information on our behalf.

These providers may include:

  • PlantNet for flower identification
  • Apple App Store and Apple In-App Purchases
  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Analytics and crash reporting providers

These providers process information according to their own privacy policies.

In-App Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store payment card information.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the app and fulfill legitimate business purposes.

If you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymize personal information within a reasonable period unless retention is required by law.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Request a copy of your information
  • Object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Ok Bloomer is not directed toward children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to help protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes become effective when posted within the app or on our website.
