Position before polish

A clear position decides what the mark, deck, site, motion, and product story should do. I use design to make that position impossible to miss.

02

Complex-product fluency

AI platforms, maps, enterprise software, marketplaces, and new categories need translation. I make the useful thing feel obvious without flattening the weirdness that makes it valuable.
