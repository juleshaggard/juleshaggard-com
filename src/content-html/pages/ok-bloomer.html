Field journal first

Made for curious walks, not desk research.

Ok Bloomer treats identification as the beginning of the experience. After a photo is verified, the app turns it into a mapped observation, a journal entry, and progress toward a regional collection.

The result is part flower identifier, part personal archive, and part collecting game. It gives people a reason to notice what is blooming around them, then keep going.

01

Collect flowers around you

See nearby blooms on a map, add verified finds, and watch your field record build around the places you actually visit.

02

Build your living field guide

Search species by rarity, season, common name, or scientific name, then save confirmed observations into your own collection.

03

Learn as you collect

Each entry keeps the useful context close: taxonomy, species notes, source photos, and the confidence that comes from a verified observation.

04

Celebrate every discovery

Badges, patches, and collection progress make small finds feel worth saving, from the first park stroll to a full season in bloom.
